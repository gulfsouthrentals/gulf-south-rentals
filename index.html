About Us

Locally owned and easy to book

Gulf South Rentals provides affordable kayak rentals for families, couples, fishermen, and anyone looking to enjoy the outdoors.

Every rental includes a paddle and properly sized life jacket. We keep the booking process simple so you can spend more time on the water. A $30 refundable damage deposit will be added to every rental.

Every rental includes

  • Kayak paddle
  • Life jacket
  • Basic safety instructions
  • Full-day rental period
